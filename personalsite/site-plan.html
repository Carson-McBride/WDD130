Home page

Home Page Welcome Small business owner? What steps are you taking to drive online traffic to your business? Social media is on the forefront for marketing and advertisement. As a small business owner, you want to get in on the action but are already carrying a wide variety of responsibilities and have a hard time tackling the social media game. Here at Atlas Media, we create and carry your online business needs through a catered approach. Getting you started and maintaining your online presence through social media and digital marketing. Content creator? We understand how difficult it is to find long lasting work that utilizes your talents in creativity. With Atlas Media, we pair you with a variety of small businesses allowing you to perform a variety of tasks while networking with those making a difference! Alongside job opportunity, we offer certification and continued education that will keep you relevant and on top of the needs of today’s growing online needs. Who We Are Carson McBride (founder) worked for a year as an Account Executive at a recent start-up company. Wearing the many hats that come along with working at a small business alongside his exposure to other small companies in and out of the industry, he quickly realized that many small business owners understand the need to utilize social media, but don’t have the faintest clue if it isn’t directly related to their industry. Alongside small business owners, many recent graduates having grown up around social media pursued education in social media marketing but are left without work. To connect the two, Atlas Media was born. Acting as a gig provider for creators as well as an affordable solution to small business owners in properly utilizing social media for their business. Contact Us Reach out over email for information regarding how to utilize our services! carsonmcbride.ya@gmail.com

For Small Business Owners

Small Business Owner Services Social Media Campaigns With a portfolio of experience running marketing campaigns online, we can not only run, but teach your company how to operate independent of us. Utilizing tools like the “Meta Business Suite” we run advertisement campaigns on a variety of different social networks that will put your business in front of your buyer persona; allowing you to maximize exposure with minimal costs. A big feature of these tools is return information of success rates among specific demographics. This is an especially attractive feature for new startups as they test the waters building their ideal customer profile. Content Creation Employing only the best marketing content creators, we will bring your ideas to life! Appealing to any needs your business has. Using industry leading software and tools, our high quality content will set you above the rest. Our creators diverse backgrounds and talents can streamline the pipeline of content creation. Beginning with an idea, moving it through fulfilling, editing, revising, all the way through publishing under the right circumstances to maximize exposure. Account Management Maintaining social media campaigns, advertisements, and new content to put out can be intimidating to maintain. We offer a variety of long-term plans to fit your needs that leave you at the helm without all the time consumption and stress that comes with maintaining an online presence.
